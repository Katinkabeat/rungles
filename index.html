How to Play Rungles

The goal

Build a ladder of connected words. Each word is a "rung." The solo game is 7 rungs; multiplayer is 10.

Each rung

  • You have a rack of 7 letter tiles (standard Scrabble distribution).
  • Your word must be 4 to 7 letters and in the dictionary.
  • Rung 1: use any letters from your rack.
  • Rung 2 and onward: your word must use at least 3 letters from the previous word. These "carried" letters are shown in teal and are free — they don't consume your rack tiles.
  • Your rack refills back to 7 tiles after every rung.

Scoring

  • Only rack letters score. Carried letters are free, but earn no points.
  • Length bonus: +2 points per letter beyond 4.
  • 2× slot: each rung has one premium position (shown as a gold slot before you play). If a rack letter lands there, its value is doubled. Carried letters don't trigger it.

Example

Rung 1: STORE → Rung 2: TORCH (T, O, R carried from STORE; C and H from rack).

Tiles

  • Blanks are wildcards. Pick any letter the first time you play one — it's locked to that letter forever.
  • The opening rack is always playable (at least one valid word exists).

Placing tiles

  • The word area has 7 fixed slots — one per letter of your word.
  • Tap a tile in your rack or carried row to pick it up (it lifts and glows).
  • Tap any slot in the word area to drop the tile into that slot.
  • If the slot is already filled, the new tile takes its place and the old one goes back to the rack.
  • Tap a filled slot with nothing selected to send that tile back to the rack — the slot becomes empty until you fill it.
  • Tap two rack tiles in sequence to rearrange your rack (the first jumps to the left of the second).
  • Tap the same tile twice to deselect.
  • You can't submit a word with an empty slot in the middle — fill every slot up to the length of your word first.

Buttons

  • Submit: play your word.
  • Clear: empty the word area.
  • Shuffle: reorder the rack randomly.
  • 💡 Hint (−5): reveal one word you could play. Score can go negative.
  • Give Up: end the game. Double-click to confirm.

Keyboard

Type a letter to pick it (carried first, then rack). Backspace removes the last tile. Enter submits. Escape clears.

Multiplayer (when it's live)

  • Two players share one ladder. Each has their own rack (hidden from the other); the tile bag is shared.
  • You take turns adding rungs — 10 total, 5 each.
  • Only your own rung scores count toward your total.
  • If you can't form a valid word, you can Skip; the other player keeps going.
